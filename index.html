Calculation Formula

This is a barcode checksum calculator that follows the following formula:

Odd digits (EAN-13 Barcode)

Separate: 940054700987

Operate:
9 + 0 + 5 + 7 + 0 + 8 = 29
4 + 0 + 4 + 0 + 9 + 7 = 24

Calculate:
3 x 4 = 12
2 + 9 = 11
+ 1 = 10
= 9

Even digits (UPC-A Barcode)

Separate: 75318295342

Operate:
5 + 1 + 2 + 5 + 4 = 17
7 + 3 + 8 + 9 + 3 + 2 = 32

Calculate:
3 x 2 = 6
6 + 7 = 13
+ 3 = 10
= 7
